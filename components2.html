HARDWARE COMPONENTS

  • I.A humidity sensor is a device that measures the amount of moisture or water vapor in the air. It is commonly used in weather stations, HVAC systems, and industrial applications to monitor and control the humidity level.
  • II. Jumper wires are typically used in electronics and prototyping to create connections between components on a breadboard or circuitboard. They come in various lengths, colors, and types, such as male-to-male, male-to-female, and female-to-female, providing flexibility in wiring configurations.
  • III.A breadboard is a tool used for prototyping electronic circuits without the need for soldering. It consists of a grid of holes where components can be inserted and connected by jumper wires.
