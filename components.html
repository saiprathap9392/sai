HARDWARE COMPONENTS

  • I.The Arduino Uno is a popular microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P chip, widely used for prototyping and DIY projects. It features digital and analog I/O pins, a USB connection for programming, and can be easily expanded with shields for additional functionality.
  • II. A soil moisture sensor is a device that measures the moisture level in the soil. It helps optimize watering schedules for plants by providing real-time data on soil moisture content, preventing both over-watering and under-watering.
  • III.A temperature sensor measures the temperature of its environment and converts it into a numerical value. It is used in various applications for monitoring and controlling temperature conditions.
  • IV.A light sensor detects the presence and intensity of light in its environment. It converts this information into an electrical signal for use in various applications such as automatic lighting control and brightness adjustment in displays.
